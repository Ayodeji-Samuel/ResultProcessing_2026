## For HoD (Head of Department)

### Start of Semester

**1. Create User Accounts**
- Click "Users" â†’ "Create User"
- Fill in details
- Assign role (Lecturer or Level Adviser)
- For Level Advisers: Assign level & program

**2. Assign Lecturers to Courses**
- Go to "Courses"
- Click "Assign" button next to each course
- Select lecturer(s)
- Save assignment

### During Semester

**Monitor Result Uploads**
- View all courses
- Check approval status
- Track progress

### Corrections Needed

**Unlock Approved Results**
1. Go to course view page
2. Click **"Unlock Results"** button (Amber)
3. Notify lecturer to make corrections
4. Wait for lecturer to re-approve

### After Departmental Board Meeting

**Give Final Approval**
1. Ensure all results are locked (lecturer-approved)
2. Go to each course view page
3. Click **"Give Final Approval"** button (Green)
4. Confirm approval
5. âœ… Course is now **OFFICIALLY APPROVED**

## Troubleshooting

### "Access Denied" Error
**Cause**: Not assigned to course  
**Solution**: Contact HoD to assign you

### Can't Find Course
**Cause**: Not in your assigned level/program  
**Solution**: Verify assignment with HoD

### "Results Locked" Message
**Cause**: Already approved  
**Solution**: Request HoD to unlock

### Upload Failed
**Cause**: CSV format incorrect  
**Solution**: Download sample CSV and follow format
